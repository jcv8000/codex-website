Getting Started


Create a notebook by clicking the icon next to "NOTEBOOKS" on the sidebar, or by pressing Ctrl+N
Then right-click on the notebook and choose "New Page".




From there you can start writing your notes.


Other Useful Information


  • Codex automatically saves pages when you switch to another page, and when you exit the program. You can manually save by pressing Ctrl+S
  • Codex also automatically checks for updates when you open the program.
  • The sidebar can be resized, and can be toggled by using the button, with (View > Toggle Sidebar) or Ctrl+D
  • While editing notes, you can zoom in/out with Ctrl++ and Ctrl+- or (View > Zoom in/out).
  • If it seems like something isnt working right, check the Developer Console for errors by pressing Ctrl+Shift+I or going to (View > Toggle Developer Tools)
